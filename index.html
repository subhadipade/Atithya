Every part of your Atithya day is planned so you bring home beautiful memories—never the tiredness. Here are our three simple promises to you:

OUR STORY

For years, we spent our Pujas wandering through Kolkata's quiet lanes, completely in love with the magic of Old Bonedi Baris. There is something so special about standing in a historic, sunlit courtyard and feeling the long history living around you. One day, we looked at each other and thought: why keep these moments just to ourselves? We realized how exhausting the festival crowds, traffic, and long queues can be, especially if you want to share these moments with elders or family. That is why we started Atithya. We wanted to open the doors to these private family heritages and share the nostalgic, peaceful side of Puja with you—completely stress-free.
